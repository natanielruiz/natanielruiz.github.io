About me

I am a Research Scientist at Google. Previously I completed my PhD at Boston University, advised by Professor and Dean of the College of Arts and Sciences Stan Sclaroff. My primary research focus is computer vision and machine learning.

I interned at Amazon, Apple AI Research, and NEC-Labs, among others. I have been honored with awards such as the Student Best Paper Honorable Mention at CVPR 2023.

Research

Currently, my main interests include generative models, diffusion models, and adversarial attacks.
